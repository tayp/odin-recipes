Katogo

Katogo. A spicy ugandan meal

Katogo is made up of peeled matooke fingers, onions, tomatoes, garlic, some curry powder,
a little salt and a vegetable of your choice all together mixed well with water and cooked for close to half an hour.

Ingredients

  • Matooke fingers
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic & Curry poweder
  • Water & Salt
