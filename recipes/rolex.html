Rolex

Rolex. A ugandan favorite snack.

Rolex is made up of chapati, fried eggs, onions, tomatoes, green pepper
and cabbage. The eggs are fried together with the other ingredients except chapati.
The result is then wrapped up in a chapati.

Ingredients

  • Chapati
  • Two eggs
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Green pepper & cabbage
  • Water & Salt
